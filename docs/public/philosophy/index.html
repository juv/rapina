Philosophy

Rapina is opinionated by design. This section explains why we made certain choices.

Good documentation doesn't just teach people how to use a project. It filters who chooses to use it.

The goal isn't to attract everyone. It's to attract developers who understand and agree with the direction Rapina is taking.
